At Symptoma, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Symptoma mobile application. We comply with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other relevant laws in jurisdictions where we operate.

3. Scope and Purpose

This Privacy Policy applies to the Symptoma app, a mobile application that provides AI-powered medical guidance. The app does not require any account registration, and processes all input locally or in-memory through secure, encrypted channels. No user profiling or persistent identifiers are used.

4. Information We Do Not Collect

Symptoma does not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII). This includes:

  • Name, surname, or email address
  • IP address, device ID, or location
  • Clinical, biometric, financial, or behavioral data
  • Any unique identifiers or analytics profiles

5. Use of Entered Information

The information you enter (e.g., symptoms or comments) is processed securely in real-time to generate a chatbot response using OpenAI’s API. This data:

  • Is not linked to any user profile
  • Is not stored on our servers
  • Remains on your device and is deleted when chat history is cleared or the app is uninstalled

6. Cookies and Tracking

Symptoma does not use cookies, tracking scripts, analytics SDKs, or advertising technologies.

7. Data Sharing and Transfers

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third party.

If you click a medical link or article referenced in a chatbot response, you may be redirected to an external site. These sites have their own privacy policies and are outside Symptoma’s control.

8. Your Rights

Because Symptoma does not store or process personal data, the rights commonly granted under privacy laws (such as GDPR and CCPA) — including access, correction, erasure, and portability — do not apply.

You may still contact us at info@panther.ad to confirm that no personal data is collected or retained.

9. Children’s Privacy

Symptoma is not intended for children under 18 years of age (or the minimum legal age in your jurisdiction).

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has used the app and entered personal data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

10. Security Measures

  • All chat history is stored locally on your device in sandboxed, app-restricted memory.
  • It is not uploaded to cloud services
  • Other apps or users cannot access this data
  • Uninstalling the app or clearing chat history will permanently delete all stored content

11. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in app functionality or legal requirements. Material changes will be communicated through the app. Continued use after notice implies acceptance of the revised policy.
