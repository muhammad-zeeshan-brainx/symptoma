3. Scope and Purpose

This Privacy Policy applies to the Symptoma app, a mobile application that provides AI-powered medical guidance. The app does not require account registration and processes input either locally or via secure, encrypted channels. No persistent user profiles or unique tracking identifiers are used.

4. Information We Do Not Collect

Symptoma does not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII), including:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact details
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or geolocation
  • Clinical, biometric, financial, or behavioral data
  • Any unique analytics or advertising identifiers

5. Use of Entered Information

Text entered by the user (e.g., symptoms or health-related concerns) is processed securely to generate chatbot responses using OpenAI's API. This data:

  • Is not linked to any user profile
  • Is not stored on external servers
  • Remains entirely on your device and is deleted if chat history is cleared or the app is uninstalled

6. Cookies and Tracking

Symptoma does not use:

  • Cookies
  • Third-party analytics SDKs
  • Advertising or marketing trackers

Your use of the app is anonymous and unmonitored by the app provider.

7. In-App Purchases and Subscription Handling

Symptoma offers a Monthly Subscription Plan, processed securely via Apple or Google In-App Purchases. We do not store or process your payment information.

Subscription Terms:

  • Free Trial: All new users receive a 3-day free trial. Full access is granted during the trial period.
  • Auto-Renewal: If not canceled during the trial, the subscription automatically renews to a Monthly Plan.
  • Billing: Payment is charged to your Apple ID or Google account at confirmation. Subscription renews automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the billing period.
  • Prices: Subscription fees are shown in your local currency and include any applicable taxes. Prices are determined by Apple/Google store regions.

Cancellation & Management:

  • On Apple devices: Settings ‚Üí Apple ID ‚Üí Subscriptions
  • On Android devices: Google Play ‚Üí Profile ‚Üí Payments & Subscriptions

Refunds: All refund requests are subject to Apple or Google policies. Symptoma does not issue direct refunds or partial credits.

Restore: Purchases can be restored on other devices using the same Apple ID or Google account.

8. Data Sharing and Transfers

Symptoma does not share, sell, or transfer user data to third parties. If the user clicks a medical article or link in the chatbot, they may be redirected to an external site. Symptoma does not control or assume responsibility for external privacy practices.

9. Your Rights

As Symptoma does not collect or store personal data, the rights typically granted under data protection laws (e.g., access, rectification, erasure) do not apply. However, users may still contact us at info@panther.ad with any concerns.

10. Children's Privacy

The Symptoma app is not intended for use by children under 18 years of age, or the minimum legal age in your jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect any data from minors.

11. Security Measures

All consultation history is saved locally on the user's device in encrypted, app-only storage. This data is never transmitted to cloud servers. Users can delete all data by uninstalling the app or manually clearing chat history.

12. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in features, legal obligations, or operational practices. Material changes will be displayed within the app. Continued use after updates implies acceptance of the revised policy.
